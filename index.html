about

Hi, I'm Kasen. By day, I am a technical program manager building AI products, with an MS in AI/ML from UCLA and a BS in Mathematics and Computer Science from UCSD.

I currently lead an omnichannel agent program that fuses chat and voice into one experience, after shipping standalone voice agents and an LLM-powered analytics tool called AI Data Explorer. I like finding quiet places where AI and automation can make everyday work feel lighter.

My favorite ideas sit where finance, tech infrastructure, AI, and agents overlap, and that is where most of my side projects end up. When I close the laptop, I am usually watching markets, traveling somewhere new, cooking, swimming, or behind a camera, always up for a new place, idea, or conversation.
